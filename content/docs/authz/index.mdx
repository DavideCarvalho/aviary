**Authz** brings Laravel's Gate & Policy ergonomics to NestJS. Define abilities once as plain functions or policy classes, then check them anywhere — guards, resolvers, services — with full type inference and zero database coupling.

<Callout title="Part of the Aviary ecosystem">
Every Aviary library reads from a shared [Context](/docs/context), so adopting one
makes the next one cheaper.
</Callout>

## Install

```bash
pnpm add @dudousxd/nestjs-authz
```

## Two ways to fly

### Plug-n-play

Register `AuthzModule.forRoot()`, define a Gate, and guard a route with `@Can('update', post)`. The defaults assume nothing about your persistence layer.

### Fully configurable

Bring your own subject resolution, policy discovery and response shaping. Authz is the core; storage and ORM-aware adapters layer on top without touching your call sites.
